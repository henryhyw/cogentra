# Product Overview

Oral Verification OS helps a reviewer verify whether a learner genuinely understands and can defend submitted work.

The v1 workflow:

1. Create an assessment case.
2. Upload assignment, rubric, references, and one or more submissions.
3. Parse and normalize the source artifacts.
4. Generate a structured defense plan with claims, focus points, and targeted questions.
5. Publish an asynchronous oral verification session.
6. Capture, transcribe, and analyze student responses.
7. Synthesize reviewer-ready evidence and finalize a decision.

The product is reviewer-first, auditable, and LMS-independent. It supports report, presentation, project, and technical assignment families through a shared normalized artifact schema plus family-specific enrichers.
